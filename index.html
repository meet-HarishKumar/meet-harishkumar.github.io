About

Risk domain expertise
meets delivery command.

I'm a banking technology leader who has spent 17 years at the intersection of risk, compliance, and enterprise platform delivery. My career started in .NET development building risk reporting systems, evolved into six years as the sole architect of a bank-wide operational risk platform, and now spans directing concurrent delivery across 20+ teams for a global banking client.

What makes my profile uncommon is the full-stack view I bring — I've written the requirements, designed the architecture, led the build, and managed the delivery portfolio. I understand what a GRC platform needs to do at the regulatory layer, not just the technical one.

More recently I've been investing in AI governance and AI-enabled GRC — holding both a Claude Certified Architect (CCA-F) and AIGP designation — which I believe is the next frontier for risk platforms in banking.

I also shape competitive RFP responses for major banking technology pursuits, bringing a client-side lens to solution design that's led to a consistent win record.
