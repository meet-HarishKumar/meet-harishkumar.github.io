About

GRC platforms built from requirements.
Delivery led at scale.

Banking technology leader with 17 years across software engineering, GRC platform development, and large-scale portfolio delivery for global banking clients. Spent 5 years as lead BA and requirements architect for a bank-wide 8-module operational risk platform. Currently managing 20+ teams, 300+ professionals, and a $15M+ portfolio at Cognizant Technology Solutions. Known for operating at the integration layer — where modules, teams, and programmes connect.

A consistent thread across both phases of this career is operating at the integration layer — the point where siloed teams, modules, and programmes connect. On the ORM platform, becoming the only person who held the full picture across all 8 modules meant identifying critical gaps siloed teams had missed: where a control testing failure should trigger an action plan, where a risk event should initiate an RCSA, and how module sequencing shaped the viable MVP. On the delivery side, the same instinct applied across 20+ concurrent programmes — treating an ostensibly disconnected portfolio as a connected system where knowledge, patterns, and resources could flow across boundaries to produce compounded value.

I continue to follow governance transformation — including responsible AI and AI-enabled GRC — as areas of interest that extend my risk-platform foundation, without narrowing the story to AI governance alone.
